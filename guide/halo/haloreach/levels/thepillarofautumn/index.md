## Pillar of Autumn - Halo: Reach

### Basic Background:

The **Pillar of Autumn** is the final mission of \*Halo: Reach\*, where players must help the surviving members of Noble Team secure the ship and its crew. It culminates in the launch of the \*Pillar of Autumn\* for its journey to the Halo ringworld, where the events of \*Halo: Combat Evolved\* begin. As the final chapter in \*Halo: Reach\*, the mission is significant for both its emotional narrative conclusion and its intense action.

For detailed information on the mission, visit the [Halopedia: Pillar of Autumn](http://www.halopedia.org/Pillar_of_Autumn) page.
